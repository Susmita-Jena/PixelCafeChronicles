# 🌸 1. The Dream

Pixel Café Chronicles is a cozy pixel-art café simulation game where players restore a forgotten café, meet charming townspeople, and create meaningful memories one cup of coffee at a time.

This project isn't about building the biggest game possible.

It's about creating a relaxing experience that feels warm, polished, and memorable.

Every design decision should answer one simple question:

> **"Does this make the café feel cozier?"**

If the answer is yes, it belongs.

---

# 📖 4. Story

One rainy afternoon, you inherit your grandmother's little café in a peaceful town.

The café has seen better days.

The furniture is worn, the menu is small, and only a handful of regulars still stop by.

With every cup you brew, every customer you help, and every corner you decorate, the café slowly becomes the heart of the town once again.

This isn't a race to become rich.

It's a journey to create a place where everyone feels at home.

---

# 🌼 5. Design Philosophy

## Core Principles

- Cozy over complicated
- Quality over quantity
- Small details matter
- Beautiful UI is part of the gameplay
- Every interaction should feel rewarding
- Mobile-first without compromising desktop experience
- Clean, maintainable code

# 🔄 6. Core Gameplay Loop

```text
Open Café
      ↓
Customers Arrive
      ↓
Take Orders
      ↓
Prepare Drinks
      ↓
Serve Customers
      ↓
Earn Coins
      ↓
Upgrade Café
      ↓
Unlock Recipes
      ↓
Explore Town
      ↓
Build Friendships
      ↓
Sleep
      ↓
Next Day
```

The gameplay should encourage players to relax rather than rush.
